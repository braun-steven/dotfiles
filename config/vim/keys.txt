/------------------------------------------------------------------------------\
| Geneal       |                                                               
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| A            | Insert at line end (=$i)
| f<char>      | Jump to next <char> 
| F<char>      | Jump to previous <char> 
| ;            | Repeat last f/t movement
| ct<char>     | Cut till <char>
| mx           | Toggle mark 'x'
| dmx          | Delete mark 'x'
| 'x           | Go to mark 'x'
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| EasyMotion   |
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| <L><L>f<char>| Jump to <char>
| <L><L>w      | Jump to word
| <L><L>h/j/k/l| Jump left/up/down/right
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| Vim-Surround |
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| cs([         | Change surrounding from ( to [
| ds(          | Delete surrounding (
| ysiw]        | Surround word under cursor with []
| yss)         | Surround entire linke with ()
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| Buffers      |                                                               
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| <Leader>b    | Show buffers                                                  
| <Leader>n    | Buffer next                                                    
| <Leader>p    | Buffer previous
| <Leader>0-9  | Go to buffer 0-9
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| Misc         |                                                               
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| <Leader>f    | FZF File
| <Leader>t    | FZF Tag
| <F5>         | Toggle NERDTree
| <F6>         | Toggle TagBar 
\------------------------------------------------------------------------------/
