/------------------------------------------------------------------------------\
| Geneal       |                                                               
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| A            | Insert at line end (=$i)
| f<char>      | Jump to next <char> 
| F<char>      | Jump to previous <char> 
| ;            | Repeat last f/t movement
| ct<char>     | Cut till <char>
| mx           | Toggle mark 'x'
| dmx          | Delete mark 'x'
| 'x           | Go to mark 'x'
| q<char>      | Record in <char>
| @<char>      | Replay record from <char>
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| EasyMotion   |
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| <L>f{char}   | Jump to {char}
| <L>w         | Jump to word
| <L>j         | Jump line down
| <L>k         | Jump line up
| s{char}{char}| Jump to {char}{char}
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| Vim-Surround |
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| cs([         | Change surrounding from ( to [
| ds(          | Delete surrounding (
| ysiw]        | Surround word under cursor with []
| yss)         | Surround entire linke with ()
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| Misc         |                                                               
|--------------|----------------------------------------------------------------
| <Leader>f    | FZF File
| <Leader>t    | FZF Tag (Buffer)
| <Leader>T    | FZF Tag (Global)
| <Leader>l    | FZF Line 
| <F5>         | Toggle NERDTree
| <F6>         | Toggle TagBar 
\------------------------------------------------------------------------------/
